The Wilbur Group is a full service general contracting company based in Hull, Massachusetts. We are a second generation family owned company with an A+ score accredited by the Better Business Bureau. With a wide array of past work in commercial development, residential buildings, and interior remodeling, you can trust that we are experienced professionals.

We are equipped to handle projects from design to building development to project management. From reconstruction after climate-related incidents to large multi-family building construction, our first priority is delivering quality work to our clients. No matter the circumstances we are ready to handle your next building project, large or small. Most of our new clients are referrals from previous clients but if you are a new customer looking to remodel your home, start construction on a new building, or continue and ongoing renovation, please contact us for a consultation.
